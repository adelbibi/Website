Adel Bibi

I am a PhD student at King Abdullah University of Science & Technology (KAUST) located on the Red Sea at Thuwal in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. I'm part of a big diverse group Image and Video Understanding Lab (IVUL) which is part of the Visual Computing Center advised by Professor Bernard Ghanem. I'm interested in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Optimization. Specifically, I'm looking into RGB/RGBD trackers, Circulant Matrices for Tracking, Correlation/Convolution Filters, Stereo based Trackers, Underwater Color Correction, and Sparse Representations.
